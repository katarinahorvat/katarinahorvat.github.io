About me

I AM A DESIGNER

Hi! I'm Katarina Horvat. A young designer at the beginning of her career. I am currently a final year student at the the University North, studying Multimedia, design and application. After I told you the main thing, let's take a look at my history. I knew from a young age that I wanted to do creative work, my head was always full of ideas and with the desire to create. Also, I was attracted to marketing and the principle of marketing activities, especially digital marketing, which is making great progress with the development of social networks and the virtual world around us. So here I am, I graduated in Entrepreneurship Economics at the Faculty of Organization and Informatics of the University of Zagreb, and the profession of Multimedia and Graphic Technologies Engineer is in the making.

My primary interests are UI/UX design, graphic design and general design of digital products. Also, I have marketing skills, analytical skills and critical thinking skills, which I consider a bonus when creating design solutions. I have work experience in the field of graphic design and, in addition to freelancing, I participated in the creation of graphic solutions for larger projects with a team of design experts. Design is my first love but marketing and business skills makes my design better to follow the needs and wishes of the users.
